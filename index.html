Frequently Asked Questions

What level of English do I need to join?

All levels are welcome! Nuvy is a conversational club, not a formal class. Our goal is to make you feel comfortable speaking, whether you're a beginner or advanced. We focus on fluency and confidence.

How long is each session?

Each session is typically 90 minutes long. This gives everyone plenty of time to talk, participate in activities, and socialize.

Where are the sessions held?

We host our sessions in comfortable and convenient locations in Amman. The exact location for each session will be announced in our WhatsApp group and on Instagram.

How do I pay the 8 JDs fee?

Payment is simple! You can pay in cash when you arrive at the session. There's no need to pay in advance.

What if I'm shy or nervous about speaking?

That's completely normal, and you're in the right place. Our professional guides are trained to create a welcoming, zero-pressure atmosphere. You can speak as much or as little as you want, and we guarantee you'll feel more confident by the end!

Ready to Start Talking?

Your first conversation is just a click away. Fill out the form, and we'll see you at the next session!
