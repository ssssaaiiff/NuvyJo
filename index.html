Frequently Asked Questions

Where are the sessions held?

Our sessions are primarily held in a central, comfortable location in Amman. Specific details are provided to registered attendees via the WhatsApp group.

Do I need to sign up for multiple sessions?

No commitment required! You pay per session (8 JDs). You are welcome to join whenever your schedule allows. We just ask that you RSVP via the form to secure your spot.

What if my English level is low?

Nuvy is perfect for all levels. Our facilitators ensure every participant gets time to speak, regardless of their fluency. The goal is practice, not perfection!

How long is each session?

Each Nuvy session typically lasts between 90 minutes to 2 hours, focusing on maximum conversation time for everyone.
